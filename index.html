Aerospace Club

This is our club.

Our mission is to create a club that is able to learn about and launch drones as well as rockets

Divisons

We have three divsions:
Our first divsion is the Rocketry Divison. It will encompass all rocket launches that our club preforms.
Our second divison is the Fixed-Wing Drone Division. It will encompass all fixed-wing drone launches that our club preforms.
Our last divison is the Rotary Drone Divison. It will encompass all rotary drone launches that our club preforms.
